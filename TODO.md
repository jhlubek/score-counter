# Future Game Candidates

Potential games to add as score counters. Ordered roughly by popularity in Czech pubs.

## High Priority

- **Mariáš** — Czech national card game. 3 players. Complex bidding and scoring (sedma, kilo, betl, durch). Scoring is painful to track manually, making it a strong candidate.
- **Lóra (Lórum)** — 4 players. 8 different mini-games/contracts scored differently. A nightmare to track on paper.
- **Tisíc (1000)** — 3 players. Race to 1000 points with bidding system and trick scoring.

## Medium Priority

- **Sedma** — 2-4 players. Tracking tens and aces taken, simple point tallying.
- **Prší** — 2-6 players. Simple penalty point tracking across rounds.
- **Žolíky / Rummy** — 2-4 players. Counting leftover card points each round.
- **Kanasta** — 2-4 players. Complex meld scoring with running totals.

## Lower Priority

- **Durák** — 2-6 players. Last man standing, tracking who's the "fool" across games.
- **Oko / Jednadvacet (Blackjack)** — 2-8 players. Tracking wins/bets across rounds.
